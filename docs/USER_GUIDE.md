# User Manual

Welcome to the Remote Worker Tracker System. This guide walks employees through
everyday tasks.

## Signing in

1. Go to your company's RWT URL.
2. Enter your **email (or username)** and **password**.
3. Tick **Keep me signed in** on trusted devices.
4. If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the 6-digit code from your
   authenticator app.

Forgot your password? Click **Forgot password?** and follow the emailed link.

## The dashboard

Your dashboard adapts to your role and shows the KPIs most relevant to you —
hours worked today, open tasks, productivity and unread notifications — plus a
7-day attendance trend and quick actions.

## Navigation

- **Sidebar** — jump between modules. Collapse it with the ☰ button.
- **Top bar** — global search, live clock, light/dark toggle 🌙, notification
  bell 🔔 and your profile menu.
- **Theme** — click the moon/sun icon to switch between light and dark mode; your
  choice is remembered.

## Attendance

- **Clock In / Out** — open **Attendance** and use the big clock widget. The
  timer shows your elapsed working time.
- **Breaks** — start and resume breaks with one click; break time is excluded
  from worked hours.
- **Leave** — **Attendance → Request Leave**, choose a type and dates, and
  submit. Track approval status under **Leave Requests**.

## Time tracking

- **Timer** — open **Time Tracking**, type what you're working on and press
  **Start**. Press **Stop** when done.
- **Manual entry** — add past work via **Add Manual Entry**.
- **Timesheets** — entries roll up into weekly timesheets. Submit them for
  manager approval.

## Tasks

- **Board** — a Kanban board of your tasks. **Drag cards** between columns to
  update their status.
- **Task detail** — add comments, tick checklist items and see activity.
- **Create** — **New Task**, fill in the details and assign teammates.

## Productivity

See your daily productivity, activity and focus scores, a 14-day trend and how
you compare on the team leaderboard.

## Notifications

The 🔔 bell shows unread counts. Open **Notifications** to read everything, or
**Preferences** to choose which categories and channels (in-app / email) you
receive.

## Your profile & security

- **My Profile** — update your name, photo, phone, timezone and theme.
- **Security** — change your password, enable **two-factor authentication** and
  review/revoke active sessions and recent sign-ins.

## Tips

- Pages work on mobile — the layout adapts to small screens.
- Use **global search** (top bar) to jump straight to a person, project or task.
- Destructive actions ask for confirmation before proceeding.
